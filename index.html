About Me

I am a Data Scientist and Machine Learning Engineer with practical experience in developing intelligent systems across computer vision, gesture recognition, and real-time data pipelines. I specialize in taking raw, unstructured data and converting it into high-accuracy predictive models and automated decision-making systems.

My expertise covers the entire machine learning lifecycle—from dataset creation and feature engineering to model training, hyperparameter tuning, and deployment-ready optimization. I have built multiple solutions involving hand landmark extraction, gesture segmentation, temporal smoothing, and real-time inference, enabling fast and stable predictions even under challenging conditions such as variable lighting, fast movement, or multiple gesture transitions.

I actively explore modern research in computer vision, including MediaPipe-based hand and pose detection, deep learning architectures for gesture recognition, and advanced tracking algorithms. My work includes designing end-to-end ML pipelines, benchmarking models, implementing accuracy improvements, and improving system responsiveness through optimized preprocessing and post-processing.

Beyond core machine learning, I focus on integrating data science solutions into functional applications, creating real-time dashboards, UX components for ML outputs, and automation pipelines. This combination of technical depth and practical system design allows me to deliver solutions that are not only accurate, but also user-friendly, efficient, and ready for real-world use.
